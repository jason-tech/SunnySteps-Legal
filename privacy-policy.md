# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 26, 2026

SunnySteps (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the SunnySteps mobile application (the “App”).

---

## 1. Overview

SunnySteps is designed to support parents and caregivers through guided activities and insights.

We aim to **minimize data collection** and protect your privacy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### a. Information You Provide

The App may store information you enter, such as:

- Child’s name  
- Child’s age  
- Communication level  
- Selected focus areas or challenges  
- Activity results or progress data  

This information is used **only within the App** to personalize your experience.

---

### b. Automatically Collected Information

We may collect limited technical data to improve the App, such as:

- App usage events (e.g., feature interactions, navigation)  
- Device type and operating system version  
- Basic performance data (e.g., crashes, errors)  

This data does **not directly identify you or your child**.

---

## 3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Generate daily missions and activity plans  
- Provide insights about progress  
- Improve app performance and user experience  
- Understand general usage patterns  

We do **not** use your data for advertising targeting.

---

## 4. Data Storage

- Data may be stored **locally on your device**  
- Some data may be processed using secure cloud services to support app functionality  

We take reasonable measures to protect your data.

---

## 5. Sharing of Information

We do **not sell, rent, or trade your personal information**.

We may share limited data only:

- With service providers that help operate the App (e.g., analytics, infrastructure)  
- When required by law  

All third-party services are expected to handle data securely.

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy

SunnySteps is intended for **parents and caregivers**, not for direct use by children.

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

Any child-related information entered into the App is provided by the parent or caregiver.

---

## 7. Subscriptions and Payments

All payments are handled by the Apple App Store.

We do not collect or store:

- payment information  
- credit card details  

Please refer to Apple’s privacy policies for billing-related data.

---

## 8. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the App’s functionality.

You can remove app data at any time by:

- deleting the App  
- clearing app data through your device settings (if available)  

---

## 9. Your Choices

You can:

- choose what information to enter  
- stop using the App at any time  
- delete the App to remove locally stored data  

---

## 10. Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information.

However, no system can be completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Continued use of the App means you accept the updated policy.
